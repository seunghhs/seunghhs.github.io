Research Interests

  • **High-Efficiency Material Screening and Discovery:** Accelerating advanced material discovery through the integration of high-throughput virtual screening (HTVS) combined with molecular simulations, and machine learning (ML).
  • **Machine Learning-Based Structure-Property Relationship Elucidation:** Exploring complex relationships between the structural characteristics and performance metrics of materials using sophisticated machine learning models, particularly Transformer models.
  • **AI-Driven Novel Material and System Design:** Inverse design and synthesis of novel materials applying generative models, such as diffusion models and transformer models.
  • **Data Mining and Knowledge Extraction using Large Language Models (LLMs):** Developing methodologies that leverage Large Language Models (LLMs) to automatically collect and analyze vast amounts of material properties and synthesis condition data from scientific literature.
  • **Multiscale Simulation and Mechanism Interpretation:** Elucidating complex behaviors and mechanisms within MOFs, polymers, catalysts, and battery systems through density functional theory (DFT) and molecular dynamics (MD) simulations.
