Research Interests

           
               
                   

1. High-Efficiency Material Screening and Discovery

Accelerating advanced material discovery through the integration of high-throughput virtual screening (HTVS) combined with molecular simulations, and machine learning (ML).

                   

2. Machine Learning-Based Structure-Property Relationship Elucidation

Exploring complex relationships between the structural characteristics and performance metrics of materials using sophisticated machine learning models, particularly Transformer models.

                   

3. AI-Driven Novel Material and System Design

Inverse design and synthesis of novel materials applying generative models, such as diffusion models and transformer models.

                   

4. Data Mining and Knowledge Extraction using Large Language Models (LLMs)

Involved in developing methodologies that leverage Large Language Models (LLMs) to automatically collect and analyze vast amounts of material properties and synthesis condition data from scientific literature.

                   

5. Multiscale Simulation and Mechanism Interpretation

Elucidating complex behaviors and mechanisms within MOFs, polymers, catalysts, and battery systems through density functional theory (DFT) and molecular dynamics (MD) simulations.
