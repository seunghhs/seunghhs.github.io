About Me & Education

           
                               
SH
               
                   

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at KAIST , specializing in the application of advanced **Machine Learning (ML) techniques** for **Materials Informatics**. My research focuses on accelerating material discovery through the inverse design and virtual screening of novel materials like **Metal-Organic Frameworks (MOFs)** and **Polymers**, primarily leveraging models such as **Transformers** and **Generative Models**.

Core Research Interests

           
                               
                   
                       
🔍
                       

High-Efficiency Material Screening & Discovery

                   
                   
                        HTVS & ML                        

Accelerating discovery via High-Throughput Virtual Screening (HTVS) combined with molecular simulations and machine learning (ML).

                   
               
                               
                   
                       
🧠
                       

ML-Based Structure-Property Elucidation

                   
                   
                        Transformer Models                        

Exploring complex relationships between structural characteristics and material performance using sophisticated machine learning models, especially Transformer architectures.

                   
               
               
                   
                       
📚
                       

Data Mining with Large Language Models (LLMs)

                   
                   
                        Knowledge Extraction                        

Developing methodologies to leverage LLMs for automated collection and analysis of material properties and synthesis data from scientific literature.

                   
               
           
                   
                       
💡
                       

AI-Driven Novel Material and System Design

                   
                   
                        Inverse Design                        

Inverse design and synthesis of novel materials applying generative models, such as diffusion models and transformer models.

                   
               
                   
                       
⚛️
                       

Multiscale Simulation and Mechanism Interpretation

                   
                   
                        DFT/MD                        

Elucidating complex behaviors in MOFs, polymers, catalysts, and battery systems through DFT and MD simulations.
