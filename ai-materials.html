Research Overview

My research focuses on developing cutting-edge AI models for the inverse design and synthesis of novel materials. By integrating experimental X-ray diffraction (XRD) data with advanced transformer architectures, I'm creating systems that can predict and generate materials with targeted properties.

The EGMOF (Efficient Generation of Metal-Organic Frameworks) project combines diffusion models with transformer architectures to achieve unprecedented accuracy in property targeting. This work represents a paradigm shift in how we approach material discovery, moving from trial-and-error to AI-guided design.

🔬
Key Research Areas

🧬

XRD-Integrated Transformer

Developing transformers that directly integrate experimental X-ray diffraction patterns to enhance structural prediction accuracy and enable real-time material characterization.

🎨

Diffusion-Transformer Hybrid

Creating hybrid models that combine the generative power of diffusion models with the sequence understanding of transformers for MOF generation with targeted properties.

⚡

Property-Driven Generation

Implementing inverse design strategies where desired properties (gas adsorption, conductivity) are input to generate novel material structures automatically.
